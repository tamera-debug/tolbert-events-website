Common questions

What size events do you work with?

I typically work with events featuring 100-500 speakers, though I can support larger programs (up to 1,000 speakers) through strategic partnerships. For smaller events, my advisory services might be a good fit.

Do you work with virtual and hybrid events?

Absolutely. I work with virtual, hybrid, and in-person events equally. The speaker management and content strategy principles apply across all formats—the execution details just shift.

How far in advance should I reach out?

Ideally 6-18 months before your event, depending on scope. That said, I've helped organizations with tighter timelines when the fit is right. Reach out and let's discuss your situation.
